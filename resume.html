Sumary

Yulieth Martinez

Ph.D. candidate in Political Science at the Federal University of Minas Gerais in Brazil. She obtained a research fellowship from the German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) to finish her Ph.D. at the Graduate School of the Social and Behavioural Sciences (GSBS) at the University of Konstanz in Germany. She is a member of the Interinstitutional Research Network on Foreign Policy and Political Regime (RIPPERP), coordinated by Prof. Dr. Dawisson Belem Lopes, and the Group for International Politics member, coordinated by Prof. Dr. Dirk Leuffen.
